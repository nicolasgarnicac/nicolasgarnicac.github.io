Introduction

Pneumonia is commonly diagnosed through symptoms such as cough, fever, and x-ray imaging. The congestion of the bronchi and alveoli leads to high-intensity zones in the image, aiding diagnosis.

The Dataset

The dataset comes from Kaggle. It contains three classes:

  • Virus pneumonia
  • Bacteria pneumonia
  • Normal (No pneumonia)

Model and training performance

The model is a convolutional neural network trained on x-ray images to classify pneumonia types.
